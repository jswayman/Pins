1 · What is Pins?

Pins is a PGA Tour golf pick'em pool app. Before a tournament starts, each player selects 10 golfers from the tournament field. When the tournament concludes, your score is the combined prize earnings of all 10 golfers you picked. Highest total earnings wins the pot.

Pins supports any PGA Tour event pulled directly from the ESPN Golf API. Earnings are calculated from the tournament's official prize purse using the standard PGA payout percentage table.

Tip: Picks are locked by the host before the tournament starts — choose strategically! Picks can't be changed once the pool is locked.

2 · Getting started

Sign up

Tap Sign Up on the login screen and fill in your username, email, phone, and choose an avatar. Your account is shared across all YourGameSpot apps (GameGrid, GridIron, GameNight).

Sign in

Enter your username or email and password. If you've signed in to another YourGameSpot app on this device, Pins may sign you in automatically via shared SSO.

Forgot password

Tap Forgot password? on the login screen. Enter your email and we'll send a 6-digit reset code. The code is valid for 15 minutes.

3 · Creating a pool

  1. Tap + New Pool in the bottom tab bar.
  2. Select a PGA tournament from the list. Only upcoming and currently active tournaments appear.
  3. Give your pool a name.
  4. Set the entry fee (cash — tracked manually) and max entries per player.
  5. Choose public (visible in Browse) or private (join-by-code only).
  6. Set your payout split — percentages must add up to 100%. Default is 70/20/10.
  7. Tap Create Pool. Your 6-character join code is displayed immediately.
Tip: Share the join code or use the 📲 Invite button on the leaderboard screen to send a direct link to anyone.

4 · Joining a pool

By code

Tap My Pools on the home screen, enter the 6-character code in Join a Pool, and tap Join.

By link

Tap any invite link you've received (e.g. pins.yourgamespot.com?join=ABC123). You'll be taken straight to the pool.

Browse Public

Tap Browse in the bottom tab bar to see all public pools. Tap any card to view it and join.

5 · Picking your golfers

  1. Open your pool and tap + Add Entry.
  2. Browse or search the tournament field — all golfers in the ESPN field are listed with their current world ranking.
  3. Tap a golfer to select them (highlighted in gold). Tap again to deselect.
  4. Select exactly 10 golfers. The submit button activates once you've chosen 10.
  5. Tap Submit Picks. You can re-submit picks (replacing your previous ones) until the host locks the pool.
Once the host locks the pool, picks are final. You cannot add, remove, or change golfers after lock.
Strategy tip: Mix a few favourites (lower odds, safer earnings) with some mid-range picks who might have a breakout week. All 10 picks contribute to your total, so depth matters.

6 · How scoring works

Your pool score is the sum of prize earnings for all 10 golfers you selected, based on their final finishing position in the tournament.

Prize purse

The host sets the tournament prize purse (in cents). The admin may also set this from the Admin Panel. Pins uses the standard PGA Tour payout percentage table — e.g., 1st place typically earns 18% of the purse.

Ties

Tied golfers split the combined earnings of their tied positions equally, exactly as the PGA Tour does. For example, if two golfers tie for 3rd, each receives the average of the 3rd and 4th place earnings.

Cut golfers

Golfers who miss the cut earn $0 in your pool. If your picks include cut golfers, those spots contribute nothing to your total.

Live updates

The server polls ESPN every 5 minutes during active tournaments. The leaderboard refreshes automatically — pull down to refresh manually at any time.

7 · Host Dashboard

As the pool host, tap ⚙️ Manage (tab bar or pool card button) to open the Host Dashboard.

Payments tab

See every entry and their payment status. Tap Mark Paid to record a cash payment. Tap again to revert.

Payout Report tab

View the final prize breakdown by place, each winner's name, and their pool earnings. The report uses only paid entries to calculate collected pot size.

Settings tab

Lock or delete the pool. Lock picks prevents new entries and pick changes — do this before the tournament starts. Deleting a pool is permanent and removes all entry and payment data.

Host checklist: Share the join code → players submit picks → lock picks before tee-off → mark payments → check payout report after the final round.

8 · Payments & payouts

Pins is a cash pool — there is no in-app payment processing. Entry fees and payouts are handled between players directly (cash, Venmo, etc.).

  • The host collects entry fees from players.
  • After the tournament, the Payout Report shows who won and how much.
  • The host pays winners from the collected pot.
  • Mark payments in the Host Dashboard to track who has paid.
